What is our Purpose?

We Created a Home away from Home with our store. Our goal is to bring the community together with the creativity of gaming. We encourage everyone who stops by to have a great time. We also host and attend our own events along with others in our local community. The reason this place all started was to bring more happiness in our town for everyone to enjoy the atmosphere of this chill environment. So, What is our purpose you ask? Our purpose is to give someone a place to just let go and have fun.
